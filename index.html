MesAmpoulesGratuites.fr, 3 million households equipped

In June 2016, GEO PLC launched its national LED bulb distribution program: MesAmpoulesGratuites.fr. This "eco-initiative" is part of the energy saving measures provided for by law for the energy transition. It allows all French households, upon simple registration on the site and under conditions of resources, to receive at their home a personalized pack comprising 5, 15 or 25 LED bulbs. The installation of these bulbs allows a saving of 100 € on the annual lighting bill. Free for one in three households, according to the resource conditions defined by the Ministry of the Environment, the price of the pack of LED bulbs varies according to the household's resources and the quantity of bulbs desired.

What is thermal insulation?

Why insulate heating pipes?

Heat loss, enemies of energy efficiency

The hot water pipes of a building intended for heating or domestic hot water are sensitive points in terms of heat loss. In fact, the hot water produced by the collective boiler intended for the dwellings of a building passes through these pipes. Read more

A light and efficient energy renovation

Among the various energy renovation works of a building, insulation is undoubtedly the least heavy and the one which is efficient the most quickly. Insulation consists of insulating hot water or heating network installations, mainly pipes. It is possible to insulate a tank or a cistern, for example. Read more

Energy savings to save on the energy bill

The thermal insulation prevents loss of calories, or loss of heat. It therefore allows, thanks to light energy renovation work, to heat less, and therefore to consume less. Read more
